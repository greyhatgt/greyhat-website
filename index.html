Join Us for GreyHat!

We welcome all!

This club is meant for people from all backgrounds, including non-technical ones. Our club is meant to teach new concepts to help everyone be more conscious of computer security.

We also invite many companies from around the United States to network with us, and give our members exclusive job opportunities.

CTF Meetings

Mondays 7 PM

College of Computing 346

We are Georgia Tech's CTF Team: The Mad Hatters! We are always taking on new members, and are more than happy to train even those who do not want to commit to formal competitions. We compete all over the United States in a multitude of competitions against some of the best minds in the world. Even so, we consistently come out on top!

Format

This is a lab-style meeting where the CTF captain walks through challenges similar to those you find in CTF competitions.
