Getting Started with GreyHat at Piazza

Due to a bug, this tutorial cannot be completed on Piazza's mobile website

All of the demonstrations and presentations will be made available through our "Course" on Piazza. In order to gain access to the club material, you must sign up for it through Piazza

Step #1: Logging in

Go to https://piazza.com and log in

Step #2: Adding a Class

Click the drop down menu in the upper left corner of the screen where you select your classes (Labeled #1 in the picture above).

Below the list of all your classes, select the link that says "Add Another Class" (Labeled #2 in the picture above).

Step #3: Choosing a Semester

The mobile site does not currently have an "Other" Option. This is a bug in the Piazza Website.

Click the Selected Term Dropdown Menu, and select the term called "Other".

Step #4: Adding GreyHat

Type in "GreyHat" into any of the class input (Labeled #1 above). After Searching, GreyHat should appear in the menu beneath. Click the first option that appears.

If it appears, select "Join as Student" in the dialog option that appears. Then, hit the "Join Class" button (Labeled #2 above).
